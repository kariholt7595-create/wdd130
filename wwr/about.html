History

Our company started with a simple love for the river and a desire to share that experience with others. What began as a small, local operation quickly grew as more people discovered the beauty and excitement of rafting with us.

Today, we proudly offer a variety of trips for families, groups, and adventure seekers of all levels. Our experienced guides are passionate about creating meaningful experiences while keeping every trip safe and enjoyable like we always will.
